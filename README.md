# Gatimu Posho Mill - MIS Business Intelligence Portal

A full-stack Management Information Systems (MIS) conceptualization for **Gatimu Posho Mill**, a local SME in Kutus, Kenya. This project transforms academic research into a scalable, responsive digital dashboard designed to solve decision-making challenges through data-driven insights.

## 1. Project Overview
This portal is based on a comprehensive analysis of Gatimu Posho Mill's operational workflows. The goal is to migrate the business from intuition-based management to a structured MIS framework utilizing:
- **Data Warehousing:** Centralizing sales and inventory records.
- **Business Intelligence (BI):** Visualizing customer trends and stock levels.
- **Decision Support Systems (DSS):** Optimizing pricing and resource allocation.

## 2. Technical Architecture
The project follows a scalable component-based architecture with a clear separation of concerns:

### Backend (API Layer)
- **Environment:** Node.js & Express.
- **Endpoints:**
  - `/api/company`: Returns core business profile data.
  - `/api/problems`: Delivers categorized research on operational bottlenecks.
  - `/api/budget`: Provides a live feed of the KES 450,000 implementation roadmap.

### Frontend (UI Layer)
- **Logic:** Vanilla ES6+ JavaScript with asynchronous fetch requests.
- **Styling:** CSS3 utilizing **BEM (Block-Element-Modifier)** for modularity and scalability.
- **Responsive Design:** Mobile-first approach ensuring accessibility for on-site mill managers.

## 3. Key Research Findings
The integrated data reflects the following research components:
- **Hypothesis:** Implementing a centralized MIS will reduce stockouts and increase profitability by 20%.
- **Methodology:** Data gathered via structured interviews with Mr. Peter Gatimu, customer surveys (n=50), and direct site observations.
- **Implementation Plan:** A phased 12-month rollout starting with digital record-keeping and culminating in a full BI suite.
